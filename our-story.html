Our Story

The Christmas Island Tourism Board, operating under the name Christmas Island Expeditions, is dedicated to enriching travelers' experiences by offering comprehensive information, resources, and convenient trip planning tools that ignite wanderlust and consistently highlight the extraordinary allure of Christmas Island. With a focus on enhancing the island's tourism industry, which contributes significantly to its economy, Christmas Island Expeditions strives to elevate the quality of life for locals by maximizing the economic benefits of the island's tourism sector, which supports a thriving community of hospitality professionals.


The dedicated team at Christmas Island Expeditions develops and executes a strategic marketing plan, refreshed biennially, encompassing various initiatives such as advertising, publication development, collaborative promotions, customer service, public relations, international marketing, tourism product enhancement, and more. Collaborating closely with local communities, industry stakeholders, government bodies, and private enterprises, Christmas Island Expeditions implements its strategic vision to showcase the island's unique offerings and attract visitors from around the globe.


Governed by a nine-member board appointed by local authorities, the Christmas Island Tourism Board oversees the operations and initiatives of Christmas Island Expeditions, ensuring alignment with the island's tourism goals and objectives. Funding for the organization is derived from a 1.5% transient lodging tax imposed island-wide, supporting the continuous growth and promotion of Christmas Island as a premier travel destination.
